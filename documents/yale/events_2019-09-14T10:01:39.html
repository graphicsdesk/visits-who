    Yale Events Near You

    Throughout the year admissions office representatives hosts public information sessions all around the world. All are welcome to attend these free events, which typically last 60-90 minutes. Registration is recommended but not required. 

    Attending an event is a great way to learn more about Yale, but it will not affect an applicant's chances of admission. Yale does not track attendance or demonstrated interest as a factor in the undergraduate admissions process.
