    Columbia Visits You

    Undergraduate Admissions travels extensively each fall and spring to introduce Columbia University to prospective students. In addition to the many individual high school visits that take place around the world, admissions officers host public events where you can learn more about Columbia University. Please read further to learn about which programs may be coming to a city near you. We look forward to meeting you and your family!

    International admission events: Columbia admissions officers travel with other colleges and universities to a variety of locations worldwide. Admissions representatives at these joint information sessions speak about the Columbia experience, as well as provide information about the admission and financial aid process.
