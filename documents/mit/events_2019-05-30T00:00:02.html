MIT visits you: Sessions on the road

This spring we’re going on the road with Caltech, Pomona, and Yale to bring our information sessions to you! We invite you to join us at one of our upcoming sessions where you can learn about our schools—financial aid, residential and student life, research opportunities, and athletics—and about college admissions in general. Please RSVP below and let us know if you will be coming. We hope to see you there!
