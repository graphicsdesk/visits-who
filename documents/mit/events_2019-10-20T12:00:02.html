MIT visits you: Sessions on the road

Not everyone can take the time to come to MIT, so each year we do our best to come to you. This fall our fantastic admissions officers will be traveling across the country to meet students and their families, and talk about life at MIT.

Information sessions are open to the general public and typically last an hour. Topics include academics, financial aid, campus life, and our application process. There is a Q&A period after the session where you can talk directly with our admissions officers and alumni.

RSVP today!
